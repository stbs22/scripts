Linear search
Best-case performance: O(1)
Worst-case performance: O(n)
complexity: O(n)

Binary search
Best-case performance: O(1)
Worst-case performance: O(log n)
complexity: O(log n)

Exponential search
Best-case performance: O(1)
Worst-case performance: O(log n)
complexity: O(log n)

Fibonacci search
Best-case performance: O(1)
Worst-case performance: O(log n)
complexity: O(log n)
